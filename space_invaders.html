Space Invaders

I developed a 2D game, 'Space Invaders,' in C# using the Azul Game Engine as part of my Master’s program. The Azul Game Engine was developed during the course of my Master’s studies.

My Work

  • Designed the gameplay architecture utilizing design patterns such as Observer, Visitor, Strategy, Object Pooling, and many more to enhance efficiency and performance.
  • Implemented a hierarchy architecture for collision detection and collision resolution.
  • Implemented a timer management system for efficient monitoring and triggering of timed events.
